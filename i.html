0.2. Sites of interest

The site ``Boulogne'' has low-rise buildings arranged in octagonal patterns, and a high water tower formed by three cylinders. In the IARPA challenge, it was used as dataset for the the explorer (preliminary) contest.

The following gallery shows the 47 input images cropped around the center of the water tower at size 800x600. Notice that the orientation and the ground resolution of each image is different, due to the arbitrary acquisition directions. Notice also that the dynamic range of each image is different, due to the seasons and to the cyrrus cover.
